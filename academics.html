Academics at Front Range International School

Explore our rigorous academic programs designed to prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive education that emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and global awareness. We offer a range of courses across various disciplines, including STEM, humanities, arts, and languages.

Advanced Placement (AP) Courses

We offer a variety of AP courses that allow students to challenge themselves academically and earn college credit while still in high school. Our AP program is supported by experienced faculty who provide guidance and support throughout the course.

Extracurricular Academic Opportunities

In addition to our core curriculum, we offer numerous extracurricular academic opportunities, including science fairs, debate clubs, and academic competitions. These activities help students develop leadership skills and foster a love for learning.

Contact Academic Affairs

If you have any questions about our academic programs or would like more information, please reach out to our academic affairs office at academic.affairs@frontrange.edu.
