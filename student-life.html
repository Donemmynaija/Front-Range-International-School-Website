Student Life at Front Range International School

Discover the vibrant student life and extracurricular opportunities that make our school community unique.

Clubs and Organizations

Our students have the opportunity to participate in a variety of clubs and organizations, ranging from academic clubs to cultural and interest-based groups. These activities foster leadership skills, teamwork, and personal growth.

Athletics and Sports

We offer a comprehensive athletics program that encourages students to stay active and develop their physical abilities. Our teams compete in various sports, promoting sportsmanship and school spirit.

Cultural Events and Activities

Throughout the year, we host cultural events and activities that celebrate diversity and provide students with opportunities to engage with different cultures. These events enhance students' global awareness and appreciation for cultural differences.

Contact Student Life Office

If you have any questions about student life or would like more information about our extracurricular programs, please reach out to our student life office at student.life@frontrange.edu.
