Admissions at Front Range International School

Discover how to become a part of our vibrant learning community.

Admissions Process

Our admissions process is designed to ensure that we select students who will thrive in our academic environment. We consider academic records, personal statements, and interviews as part of our holistic review.

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from previous schools
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)

Tuition and Financial Aid

We offer competitive tuition rates and a variety of financial aid options to support families in making an investment in their child's education. Please contact our admissions office for more information on scholarships and financial assistance.

Contact Admissions

If you have any questions about the admissions process or would like to schedule a campus tour, please reach out to our admissions office at admissions@frontrange.edu.
