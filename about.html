Our Mission

At Front Range International School, our mission is to foster a nurturing and challenging environment that encourages students to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Community Engagement

History of the School

Founded in 2005, Front Range International School has grown to become a leading institution in international education, known for its innovative curriculum and commitment to student success.
